Physics Insights

How It Works

  • Click "Start Free-Fall" to begin the simulation of the elevator dropping.
  • The ball inside the elevator will bounce according to the elasticity setting.
  • Adjust gravity to change how fast objects fall.
  • Modify air resistance to see how drag affects falling objects.
  • Change mass values to observe how different weights behave in free fall.
  • Watch the real-time data panels to track velocities and positions.
  • Use Pause to freeze the simulation at any moment.
